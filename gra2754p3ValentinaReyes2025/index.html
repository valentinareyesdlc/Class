DIY Dubai White Chocolate Bar

First, buy the ingredients that you'll need. Here's the list: pistachio cream, knafeh, butter, and white chocolate (or any chocolate of your preference).

In a pan, add butter. Once is melted, add the knafeh and cook it until it's golden brown and crispy.

Then, in a bowl, mix the pistachio cream with the crispy knafeh you just made. It needs to be wery well mixed as this is going to be the chocolate filling.

Now, it's time to melt the chocolate. You can melt it on the microwave and mix it every 15 seconds until completely melted, or on a low heat pot with a splash of hot water, and mix it until completely melted.

In a silicone mold, pour some of the chocolate until the whole base is covered. Then, add the pistachio cream filling you previously made, and then add more chocolate, until its completely covered. Then, put it on the fridge for a few hours.

Finally, carefully get the chocolate bar out of the silicone mold, and enjoy!
